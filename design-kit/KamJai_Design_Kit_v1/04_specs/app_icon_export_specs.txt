KamJai App Icon Export Specs
===========================

You can keep ONE master icon (vector) and export per platform.

iOS (App Store + device)
- 1024x1024 (App Store)
- 180x180 (iPhone @3x)
- 167x167 (iPad Pro @2x)
- 152x152 (iPad @2x)
- 120x120 (iPhone @2x)
(Plus any additional sizes your build pipeline requests.)

Android (Adaptive icon)
- Foreground layer: 432x432
- Background layer: 432x432
- Legacy icon: 512x512 (Play Store)

Web / PWA
- favicon.ico (16/32/48)
- 192x192, 512x512 (PWA manifest)
- 180x180 (Apple touch icon)

Export Guidance
- Keep a SAFE ZONE: no critical detail in the outer 12% of the icon.
- In retro mode, ensure the heart/letters remain legible at 48px and 24px.
